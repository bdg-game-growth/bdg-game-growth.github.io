Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how BDG Game — Information Hub ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores and discloses information when you visit or interact with our website and related pages (the "Site"). We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal data and to being transparent about our practices.

Scope

This policy covers information collected through our website (including GitHub Pages sites, HTML pages, and links to official resources) and does not apply to the privacy practices of the BDG platform operator. If you follow external links to other services, please review the privacy policy of those services separately.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal identifiers: contact email or name if you provide it when contacting support or submitting forms.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type and version, device identifiers, operating system, referring/exit pages, and pages visited.
  • Usage data: time of visit, duration on pages, clicks, and interaction patterns collected through analytics tools.
  • Communications: any messages or emails you send to us (including inquiries and support requests).

How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you when you contact us or submit information through forms.
  • Automatically through server logs and analytics technologies when you use the Site.
  • From third-party services (for analytics and hosting) that process data on our behalf.

Use of Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain and improve the Site and its content.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support.
  • To monitor and analyze usage and trends to enhance user experience.
  • To protect the Site, our users and our rights (fraud detection, security, abuse prevention).
  • To comply with legal obligations when required.

Cookies & Similar Technologies

We and our service providers use cookies, pixel tags and similar technologies to collect information about Site usage. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You may control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect Site functionality.

Essential cookies: required for basic site operation (navigation, security).
Analytics cookies: used to understand how visitors use the Site and to improve content.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for hosting, analytics and performance. These providers may process technical and usage data. Examples include analytics platforms and GitHub hosting. Such providers have their own privacy policies and may be located outside your country.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Log and analytics data may be retained for a limited period to support debugging, analytics and security.

Data Security

We implement reasonable organizational and technical measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure — if you believe your interaction with us is compromised, please contact us immediately (see "Contact" below).

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data where we no longer have a legal basis to retain it.
  • Object to or restrict processing of your personal data.
  • Data portability where applicable.

To exercise your rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.

Children

The Site is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove the information.

Links to Other Sites

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites (including official BDG resources and Telegram channels). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party site you visit.

International Transfers

Data processed by our service providers may be transferred to and stored in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure adequate protection for international transfers in accordance with applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the effective date. Material changes will be communicated where practical (for example, via a notice on the Site).

Contact

If you have questions, requests or complaints about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact:

Email: support@tcgame.co.in

Please include relevant details (page URL, date/time, description) to help us process your request efficiently.

This privacy policy applies to BDG Game — Information Hub pages and supplementary content hosted as part of our site. It does not replace or alter privacy terms of the official BDG platform or third parties.
